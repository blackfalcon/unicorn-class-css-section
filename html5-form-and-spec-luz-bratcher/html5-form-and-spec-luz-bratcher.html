Sample Bratcher Favorite: Black Bottle

Black Bottle is our favorite place to get cheap gourmet food. They serve shared plates, of which we always get broccoli blasted, potatoes cauliflower curried, and the mussels Marseilles. Their cocktails are fantastic and they also have allergy-friendly menus, making it exceptionally easy for everyone to enjoy something here.

Reservations accepted .
